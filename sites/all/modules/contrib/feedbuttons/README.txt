README.txt
==========

The feedbuttons.module enables a block which shows button-links allowing users to subscribe to your site's feed in their favorite RSS reader (Google, Bloglines, Newsgator, Pluck, etc.). Based on http://www.toprankresults.com/tools/button-maker.php.

To enable the module:

1) Upload the feedbuttons directory to your modules/ directory.
2) Enable feedbutton.module at admin > modules.
3) Define access permissions at admin > access.
4) Configure the modules' settings at admin > access > feedbuttons
5) Enable the feedbuttons block at admin > block
